Mukesh Ghimire [CV]

I am a third-year PhD student at Arizona State University. I work at the Design Informatics Lab at ASU. My research interests include game theory, reinforcement learning and optimization. My current focus is on multi-agent differential games, especially those with incomplete information on one side.

You can reach me at: mghimire(at)asu(dot)edu
