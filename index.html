I am a second-year PhD student at Arizona State University. I work at the Design Informatics Lab at ASU.

My research interests include game theory, reinforcement learning and optimization. For the past year I have been working on multi-agent differential games, especially those with incomplete information on one or both sides with applications relating to autonomous driving.

Whenever I get time, I love to escape from the city life, most likely to a National Park or the wilderness. Click here to view some of my travel diary in random.
